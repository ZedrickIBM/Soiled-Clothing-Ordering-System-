About Soiled

Soiled is a clothing brand established in 2021 during the surge of pandemic. In the span of four years, Soiled has produced a variety of products ranging from shirts to tube masks and pomade. With a growing number of clothing brands blooming in the market, Soiled aims of becoming one that delivers quality and significant impact to the industry.

Mission

  • Design and create high-quality clothing that empowers individuals to express their unique style.
  • Embrace the unconventional by offering unexpected and innovative designs.
  • Foster a sense of community by connecting with our customers and supporting local artists/causes.
  • Maintain a commitment to our principles in all aspects of our business.

Vision

To redefine the meaning of "Soiled" and transform it into a symbol of modern fashion. We envision a world where self-expression is embraced through clothing that tells a story, pushing the boundaries of conventional style.
